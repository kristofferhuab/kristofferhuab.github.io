About me

At exactly 9:43 am of the 2nd day of March in the year 1999, A legend was born (kidding). I was born. I was a cute (nohate), lovable as you can see, and enthusiastic kid. I studied elementary at SLU-LES. I was a very playful kid. But in 5th grade, I found basketball. My dad taught me how to play the game and up until now I still play. Regarding my academics back then, I really liked math. I loved solving regarding numbers. Of course, I graduated elementary and have to move on to the next chapter of my life, High school.



HIGHSCHOOL! It was the most memorable part of my life so far. I attended high school at BCNHS. First year was all about adjusting and improving myself in both basketball and in academics, I was a consistent top 10 in my highschool life. 2nd year, I started competing with other schools here in Baguio. I attended competitions like MILO Passarelle, YMCA Cup, and Miners Cup. I also competed academically, like math quiz bees. And again I never stopped improving myself

3rd year, I took it to the next level. I still competed with other schools but now it is within Cordillera Administrative Region. Some are NBTC Cup, YMCA Cup, MILO Passarelle, District meet, and my first ever Regionals CARAA meet. 4th year was the bomb. I was a champion twice, and even went to Davao to compete Nationally, competing with every region here in the Philippines. It was an unforgettable moment for me. I graduated still on the top 10 in my class. And I was recruited to be a varsity class-A here in the University of the Cordilleras





College was different, especially being a student athlete. I am currently taking up BSIT major in Enterprise Resource Planning. I manage to be in the deans list up until now. College training was very tiring. I was a consistent class-A varsity scholar for my whole 1st year. But I left the team before my 2nd year starts. Because now I want to focus on my studies.
